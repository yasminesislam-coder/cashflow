Private by design

This app has no accounts, no server, and no sync. What that means in practice:
1

Your data never leaves your device

Balances, bills, and imported statements are processed and stored entirely on this device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere, so there is nothing for anyone else to see.

2

Every install is its own private copy

Share the app file or link with anyone — a partner, a friend — and they get a blank copy with their own numbers on their own device. There is no way to see another person's data.

3

Backups are yours to hold

Export a data file to back up your numbers or move them to another device (export here, load it there). Anyone who shouldn't see your finances shouldn't have that file — treat it like a bank statement.
